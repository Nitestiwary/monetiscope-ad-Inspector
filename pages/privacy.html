Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the GPT Ad Inspector and Debugger Chrome Extension (developed by Monetiscope) handles user information.

1. Data Collection & Usage

The GPT Ad Inspector and Debugger is built with privacy in mind. We adhere strictly to the following principles:

  • No Browsing History Collected: The extension does NOT track or record your personal browsing history. It only inspects Google Publisher Tag (GPT) slot configurations on the active tab when you explicitly open the extension dashboard.
  • No Sensitive Personal Data Harvested: The extension does NOT read, store, or transmit form inputs, credit card numbers, passwords, or any other sensitive personal data.
  • Local Storage Only: Any state (such as your registration status or highlight toggles) is stored locally on your device inside your browser's secure sandbox using chrome.storage.local. No server-side synchronization is performed.

2. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share any data collected by the extension with third parties. All diagnostics scanning is done client-side in your active tab. When you click "Continue to Sign Up," you are redirected to the external Monetiscope website (https://monetiscope.com/register/) where any signup forms are governed by the website's independent privacy policy.

3. Chrome Web Store Policy Compliance

This extension is compliant with the Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy. It complies with the Single Purpose rule, requesting only minimal permissions (storage, activeTab, and scripting) necessary to inspect GPT tags and highlight containers visually on your active tab.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as extension features evolve. If we modify how permissions or data are handled, we will update the version number and prompt users transparently through the Chrome Web Store.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at Monetiscope Support.
